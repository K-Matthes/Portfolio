The Office is an American television sitcom that was adapted from the UK version of the show, led by Ricky Gervais. This documentary style comedy follows a small paper company called Dunder Mifflin, in the small town of Scranton Pennsylvania. Within the first few episodes of the show, you meet unpredictable branch manager, Michael Scott, likable Jim Halpert, quirky Pam Beesly, and many more unique and funny characters. In its early years, The Office was picked up by NBC and continued on for 8 lovable seasons. This show is considered a fan favorite, and is now featured on many streaming sites such as Peacock and Netflix.
