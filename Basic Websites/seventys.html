That 70's show is an American television sitcom that takes place in the 1970's and follows the lives of six teenagers navigating their way through high school in rural Wisconsin. That 70's Show aired from 1998 to 2006 on Fox, and starred Topher Grace, Mila Kunis, Ashton Kutcher, Danny Masterson, Laura Prepon and Wilmer Valderrama. That 70's Show was nominated for a number of awards, including 16 Primetime Emmys and Teen Choice Awards. The show ended up winning an Emmy for Outstanding Costume Design in 1999.
